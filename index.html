Lawn Care Tips

5 Ways to Keep Your Graham, TX Lawn Healthy All Year

Advice from Jasen's Lawn Service · Serving Graham, TX & surrounding areas

Living in Graham, TX means dealing with hot summers, dry spells, and the occasional winter freeze. Here's what we tell every one of our customers to help their lawns stay strong through every season.

1. Apply Pre-Emergent in Early Spring
Before weeds have a chance to sprout, get a pre-emergent herbicide down. This one treatment saves you weeks of post-weed frustration and keeps your lawn looking clean all season long.

2. Mow at the Right Height
Don't cut more than one-third of the grass blade at a time. Cutting too short stresses the grass and invites weeds. For most Texas grasses, 3–4 inches is the sweet spot in summer heat.

3. Don't Skip Fall Leaf Removal
Leaves left on the lawn through winter can smother the grass, block sunlight, and create a perfect home for pests and fungus. Schedule a cleanup in October or November before the cold sets in.

4. New Sod Needs Extra Water — But Don't Overdo It
Freshly installed sod needs consistent watering for the first two to three weeks. After roots establish, scale back to avoid root rot. Proper setup makes all the difference in long-term lawn success.

5. Call a Professional for Trees
Overgrown or damaged trees are a safety hazard. Regular trimming keeps trees healthy and protects your home from storm damage. This is not a DIY project — always use a trained crew with the right equipment.
